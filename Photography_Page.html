La fotografía es el arte, la aplicación y la práctica de crear imágenes mediante el registro de la luz, ya sea electrónicamente mediante un sensor de imagen o químicamente mediante un material sensible a la luz, como una película fotográfica.

"Photography keeps memories alive and prosperous because it freezes a scene that lasts forever. Many people have cameras just for that reason. When you take a photo, you capture not only the scene, you capture the feeling and emotion. Some people like photography as a hobby because it is very relaxing and euphoric."

Photography, in its essence, transcends mere visual documentation; it's a profound medium that encapsulates moments, emotions, and narratives frozen in time. Beyond the mere act of capturing images, photography is an art form that ignites the imagination, stirs the soul, and sparks connections across cultures and generations. At its core, photography is a testament to the human experience—a reflection of our curiosity, creativity, and capacity for expression. Through the lens of a camera, we navigate the world, seeking beauty in the mundane, finding solace in the chaos, and uncovering stories hidden in plain sight. In essence, photography is more than just a medium for visual representation; it's a testament to our shared humanity, a celebration of life's fleeting moments, and a testament to the beauty and complexity of the world we inhabit.
